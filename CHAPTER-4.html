Dear Sweetie,

Chapter 4.

Our first...

"I love you."

By this point...

Let's be honest.

"I love you."

We never talked about it.

Somehow...

We both just knew.

Everything else?

Allowed.

Calling every day?

Allowed.

Movie nights?

Allowed.

Planning our future together?

Apparently allowed.

Saying "I love you"?

Absolutely terrifying.

✦

Before I tell you how it actually happened...

I have to tell the story that happened before it.

Because otherwise this won't make any sense.

One day you were at school.

I was at church.

Which honestly isn't surprising because the church is basically an extension of my house.

We were talking like usual when you suddenly said something about how nobody would date you.

And I was like...

"Bro... you're literally so pretty. Why would nobody date you?"

Then...

You replied...

"Then date me."

...

EXCUSE ME?

WHAT?

My brain completely shut down.

I honestly thought you were joking.

Or teasing me.

So I dodged it.

I literally asked,

"Date... like for one day?"

HAHAHAHAHAHA.

I misunderstood everything.

I thought you meant hanging out as friends.

Because surely...

Surely...

There's no way you actually meant it.

✦

Truth is...

I've always hated jokes like that.

Ever since elementary school.

You know those people who say,

"Hey, she likes you."

Then the other person goes,

"Eww."

Yeah.

I hated that.

So I never wanted to assume someone actually liked me.

I'd rather believe I misunderstood than believe I was being played.

✦

Things got...

Very awkward after that.

So awkward that we barely talked until nighttime.

Then we finally decided...

Let's clear everything up.

✦

That conversation honestly changed everything.

Because for the first time...

I realized...

Your feelings were actually real.

BRO.

I WAS SHOCKED.

Like...

Actually shocked.

I didn't even read your messages first.

I made Cheszley read them.

HAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA.

I was so scared that I even asked AI to help me.

Imagine needing both your best friend and artificial intelligence just to survive one conversation.

Peak panic.

✦

Then you told me something I'll never forget.

You originally wanted to confess around November...

Maybe December.

But...

You got scared someone at college might steal me first.

HAHAHAHAHAHA.

Cutie.

Good thing you didn't wait.

Mostly with gay people.

For some reason.

I still don't know why.

They must've seen something I don't.

The actual first "I love you."

July 29.

We were talking about technology.

As usual...

You were bullying technology because apparently you're secretly eighty years old.

HAHAHAHAHAHA.

"It's never working for me!"

"Technology doesn't love you yet. Don't worry... I love you, and technology loves me... so you'll be loved."

Everything stopped.

"Wait... you loved me?"

I genuinely thought I had been obvious this whole time.

I wasn't.

Not even close.

Three words.
