Hi sweetie,

Do you remember how we first met?

I don't actually remember the exact date anymore. 😭 I just know it was sometime around April. Back then, I downloaded Purp because I wanted to practice my English and hopefully find someone to play Minecraft with. That's literally it. I wasn't looking for love or anything.

✦

Now here's the funny part.

I actually had a rule whenever I was swiping. If a profile looked weird, automatic swipe left. Especially those profiles where all you see are thirst traps. Like bro... no thanks. HAHAHAHA.

Then I saw your profile.

This memory no longer exists.

I wanted to put a screenshot of your Purp profile here.

Unfortunately, my old phone broke a few months ago, and with it went almost all of our earliest screenshots.

"Oh... she seems normal."

Looking back now... that one thought completely changed my life.

That was literally my reason for swiping right.

✦

A few days later, I randomly opened Purp again. Then I saw that we'd matched. And you were actually the one who messaged me first.

Our first conversation.

I wish I still had our first messages.

They're gone now... but thankfully the memories aren't.

Of course I replied. We talked about random things, and somehow ended up talking about tattoos.

✦

Funny enough, I didn't know until much later that you thought I was a jerk.

"I'm scared of pretty girls, and I hate myself."

Looking back... Yeah. I would've thought I was weird too.

✦

Then Purp decided to become Purp. It glitched so much that I asked if you had Instagram instead.

The move to Instagram.

Looking back... I'm really glad I asked.

Because if I hadn't, maybe our story would've ended there.

Thankfully, you said yes.

Then I discovered your first crime.

"I'm 18."

Turns out... you were still 17. 😭

✦

I still remember you telling me about your tattoo.

Coming from a strict Christian Filipino family, I was literally thinking...

"Wait... are all Europeans like this?"

HAHAHAHA.

✦

It's honestly crazy when I think about it now.

I downloaded Purp to practice English... and maybe find someone to play Minecraft.

Instead...

I found you. ❤️
