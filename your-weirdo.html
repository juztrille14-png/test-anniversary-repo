THE FIRST LIE

It started as something simple...

Dear Sweetie,

Do you remember this screenshot?

"Yo, can we be friends?"

Looking back now...

That was probably the biggest lie I've ever told you.

✦

The funny thing is...

It wasn't even intentional.

When I asked if we could be friends, I genuinely meant it.

At that time, that's really all I wanted. I wasn't looking for a girlfriend. I wasn't trying to flirt. I wasn't planning some master strategy. I just thought you were a really nice person, and honestly, your energy wasn't draining. 😭

You know those conversations where it feels like you're talking to a brick wall? Yeah... ours wasn't like that at all. Talking to you just felt... easy.

✦

And yes...

Before you say anything...

I already thought you were pretty.

Like... really pretty.

I'm not blind.

But being attracted to someone and being in love with them are two completely different things.

So no.

I wasn't simping.

"Damn... she's pretty."

That's it.

Nothing more.

Or at least...

That's what I kept telling myself.

✦

The more we talked, the more I realized something was changing.

You became the person I looked forward to talking to every day.

You somehow made practicing English feel effortless.

You were funny.

You were energetic.

You were easy to talk to.

You somehow turned the most random conversations into the best part of my day.

And little by little...

Without me even noticing...

You became prettier.

Not because you changed.

But because every time I got to know you a little more, I found another reason to admire you.

✦

At some point I remember thinking,

"Bro... what the hell is happening?"
"Am I actually starting to like her?"

HAHAHAHAHA.

I tried pretending nothing was happening.

Absolutely nothing.

Nope.

Still just friends.

Totally.

✦

Because there was no way I was going to confess.

You already know me.

I'm scared of pretty girls. 😭

Confessing?

Absolutely not.

I'd rather wrestle a bear.

Okay... maybe not a bear.

A goose.

Those things are terrifying.

✦

Besides, I kept thinking,

"Bro... don't ruin this."
"Just be happy she's in your life."
"Don't be stupid."

So I stayed quiet.

I kept calling you my friend...

Even though my heart was slowly starting to disagree.

✦

Which means...

That message...

"Yo, can we be friends?"

And your reply...

"Sure! Want to be one of my weirdos?"

Turned out to be the most beautiful lie I've ever told.

Because I never stayed just your friend.

I fell in love with you. ❤️

✦

Still...

I hope you'll find it in your heart to forgive me.

Because I had absolutely no idea that one little lie...

would eventually become the greatest truth of my life
